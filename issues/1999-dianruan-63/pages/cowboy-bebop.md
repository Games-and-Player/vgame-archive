# COWBOY BEBOP

> 彩页·中插

两页全彩动画海报，展示《COWBOY BEBOP》（星际牛仔）主要角色。

第一页（PDF 58）为三联竖幅角色半身像：艾德（Edward）、斯派克·斯皮格尔（Spike Spiegel）中间为黑白画风、朱莉亚（Julia）为彩色画风。页面底部印有英文介绍文字：

New York in 1941...
Young, high-sense jazz players came together at  + r1 +  in Harlem.
They were fed up with existing jazz plays, and desired to play freely as they'd like.
At this club opening to everyone who want to play,
they had jam sessions over again every night and competed with others.
Eventually they did create a new style of jazz. It was definitely new mode,
not just new song.
Soon after that, it became to be called  + r1 + .
Then, at the space in 2071...
Bounty hunters came together in the space ship,  + r1 + .
They had everything their own way, never cared about risks and broke up fixed style.
They'd have new dream, and later it'd become of new time.
This work, which is raw mode itself, would be called,  + r1 + .

第二页（PDF 59）为三联竖幅角色半身像：斯派克·斯皮格尔（Spike Spiegel）、菲·瓦伦蒂（Faye Valentine）、杰特·布莱克（Jet Black），均为黑白画风。

页面右下角标注：《电子游戏软件》1999年10月
